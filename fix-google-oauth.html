Fix Google OAuth for Southside RP

Problem: Google OAuth Not Enabled

Error: "Unsupported provider: provider is not enabled"

The Google OAuth provider is not enabled in your Supabase project. Follow the steps below to fix it.

Step 1: Go to Supabase Dashboard

  1. Open your Supabase project: https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/xylaoshplmsevlxzizrd
  2. Click on "Authentication" in the left sidebar
  3. Click on "Providers" tab

Step 2: Enable Google Provider

  1. Find "Google" in the list of providers
  2. Click the toggle to enable Google OAuth
  3. Fill in the required fields:

Required Google OAuth Settings:

Client ID: Your Google OAuth Client ID

Client Secret: Your Google OAuth Client Secret

Redirect URL: https://xylaoshplmsevlxzizrd.supabase.co/auth/v1/callback

Step 3: Get Google OAuth Credentials

  1. In Google Cloud Console, select your project
  2. Go to "APIs & Services" > "Credentials"
  3. Click "Create Credentials" > "OAuth 2.0 Client IDs"
  4. Application type: "Web application"
  5. Name: "Southside RP Auth"
  6. Authorized redirect URIs: https://xylaoshplmsevlxzizrd.supabase.co/auth/v1/callback
  7. Click "Create"
  8. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret

Step 4: Configure Supabase

  1. Go back to Supabase Authentication > Providers > Google
  2. Enter your Google Client ID
  3. Enter your Google Client Secret
  4. Make sure the redirect URL matches: https://xylaoshplmsevlxzizrd.supabase.co/auth/v1/callback
  5. Click "Save"
